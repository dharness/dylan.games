A Very Vaccuum Birthday

A Very Vaccuum Birthday was my first attempt at making an animation in toonboom.
My collaborator and I gave ourselves a week to write the script, storyboard and ship a fully animated video. We used Fiver for the voice-over work. Unfortunately, we did not get to the ink and colour before the deadline.

Co-created with Naila Nur.
